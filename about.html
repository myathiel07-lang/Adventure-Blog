About Me

Sharing my love for the outdoors, wildlife, and the stories nature tells.

“The best adventures are the ones that bring you closer to the wild.”

Hi, I'm Mya.

I’ve always felt at home in the woods. Whether it’s tracking deer signs, photographing wildlife, or discovering hidden places, nature has always been a huge part of my life.

This website is my way of sharing those experiences! The excitement of scouting, the thrill of the hunt, and the beauty of the animals and landscapes around us.

I hope my stories and photos inspire others to explore the outdoors, appreciate wildlife, and find the same peace and adventure I do.
